Welcome to HotTea! We are committed to protecting your privacy. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, and safeguard your information when you use our Chrome extension ("Service").

1. Information We Collect

To provide and improve our Service, we collect the following information:

A. Information You Provide

  • Feedback Information: If you choose to provide feedback upon uninstalling our extension, we may collect the reasons for uninstallation and any additional comments you provide. This helps us understand our users better and improve our product.

B. Information Collected Automatically

  • Google User Information: To use our Service, you must log in with your Google account. We collect your email address and public profile name for authentication purposes. We do not access your Google password or any other personal data from your Google account.
  • Service Usage Data: We automatically collect a unique, one-time token when you uninstall the extension to securely log the uninstall event. We may also collect your browser's user agent string to help us debug and improve compatibility.

C. Information We DO NOT Collect

We believe in data minimization and only collect what is necessary. We want to be clear about what we do not collect:

  • News Content: The content of the news articles you process is sent to the AI model for transformation but is never stored on our servers. It is processed ephemerally.
  • Browsing History: HotTea does not track your browsing history or the websites you visit.
  • Sensitive Personal Information: We do not collect any sensitive data, such as your password, financial information, or personal communications.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for the following purposes:

  • To Provide and Maintain the Service: To authenticate you as a user and manage your account.
  • To Improve Our Service: To analyze uninstall feedback and usage data to understand what our users need and to improve HotTea's features and performance.
  • To Communicate With You: If you contact us for support, we will use your email address to respond to your inquiries.

3. Data Sharing and Third Parties

We do not sell or rent your personal data. We only share information with the following third-party services as essential parts of our Service:

  • Google: For authentication services (Google OAuth). Your interaction with Google login is governed by Google's Privacy Policy.
  • Supabase: We use Supabase as our backend service provider to manage user authentication and store uninstall feedback data securely.
  • Generative AI Provider: We send news article content to a generative AI model to provide the core functionality of our Service. This content is not linked to your personal account and is not stored after processing.

4. Data Security

We take the security of your data seriously and use appropriate measures to protect it. This includes using secure HTTPS connections for all data transmission and relying on trusted services like Google OAuth and Supabase for authentication and data storage.

5. Your Data Rights

You have control over your personal information. You have the right to:

  • Access and Update: You can access and update your Google account information through Google's services.
  • Data Deletion: If you wish to delete your account information from our system, please contact us at support@hottea.app. We will process your request in accordance with applicable laws.
  • Uninstall: You can stop all information collection by uninstalling the extension at any time.

6. Children's Privacy

Our Service is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal information from children. If we become aware that we have collected such information, we will take steps to delete it.

7. Changes to This Privacy Policy

We may update this Privacy Policy from time to time. We will notify you of any significant changes by posting the new policy on this page and, if applicable, through the extension. We encourage you to review this Privacy Policy periodically.

8. Contact Us

If you have any questions or concerns about this Privacy Policy, please contact us at:
