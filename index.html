Frequently Asked Questions

What is HotTea?

HotTea is a free Chrome browser extension that uses Google Gemini AI technology to transform traditional news articles into engaging chat-style conversations, making news reading as fun and easy as chatting with friends.

Which news sites does HotTea support?

HotTea supports over 40 mainstream news websites, including Yahoo News, UDN, Liberty Times, ETtoday, SET News, TVBS, China Times, and other major Taiwanese media outlets, covering politics, society, technology, entertainment, and more.

Is HotTea free?

HotTea is completely free to use! After installation, enjoy AI news chat generation without any subscription or payment. Each user has a daily usage quota, sufficient for normal reading needs.

How does HotTea protect my privacy?

HotTea takes user privacy seriously. We only read the current news page content when you click the extension button. We do not collect your browsing history, passwords, or other sensitive information. All data processing uses encrypted connections and Google OAuth 2.0 secure authentication. For details, see our Privacy Policy.

Can I share the generated conversations?

Yes! HotTea's generated conversation content can be easily copied and shared to social media, messaging apps, or other platforms. You can share interesting news conversations with friends, making current events accessible and fun for everyone.

Why do HotTea conversations have character avatars?

HotTea integrates Wikipedia data. When real people appear in news, the system automatically fetches their Wikipedia avatars, making conversations more vivid and realistic. For fictional characters or when avatars aren't found, default icons are used.

What AI technology does HotTea use?

HotTea uses Google's latest Gemini AI model, an advanced large language model that excels at understanding complex text and generating natural, fluent conversational content, ensuring accurate and engaging transformations.

What if I encounter problems?

If you encounter any issues while using HotTea, feel free to leave a comment on the Chrome Web Store page or contact our support email at alan52027@gmail.com. We'll get back to you as soon as possible.
